Registration Instructions Guide
ITCG Vendor-Customer Registration Portal · Form V3.1
* Required field NA → Write "NA" if not applicable Uploads: PDF / JPG / PNG
1
Select Registration Type
Choose Vendor or Customer from the dropdown at the top
  • Select Vendor if you are supplying goods/services — Banking Information is mandatory
  • Select Customer if you are purchasing — banking details are not required
2
Section 1 — Legal Information
Business identity & tax registration numbers
  • Name of Vendor/Customer* — Registered company or business name
  • Industry Type* — Select from dropdown (e.g. Manufacturing / IT Services / Retail)
  • Contact No.* — 10-digit mobile or landline, no spaces
  • CIN No.* — 21-character Corporate ID. Write NA if sole proprietor/individual
  • TAN No.* — Tax Deduction Account No. Write NA if not applicable
  • GST* — 15-character GSTIN (e.g. 24ABCDE1234F1Z5)
  • PAN* — 10-character PAN (company PAN for companies, personal for individuals)
  • MSME No.* — Udyam Registration Number. Write NA if not registered
💡 For each document (GST, PAN, MSME) — select Yes to upload the certificate, or No to skip. Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, PNG.
3
Section 2 — Address Information
Billing & Shipping addresses
  • Billing Address* — Building/office + street, area, city, state code, 6-digit PIN
  • Shipping Address — Use the "Same as Billing" checkbox if delivery address is same as billing, or fill manually if different
  • State/UT Code — Use state name or numeric code (e.g. Gujarat / 24)
4
Section 3 — Banking Information (Vendors only)
Bank account details for payment processing
  • Name of Bank* / Branch Name*
  • Bank Account No.* — Full account number, no spaces
  • Bank Account Type* — Current Account (businesses) or Savings (individuals)
  • IFSC Code* — Find on cheque book or passbook (e.g. HDFC0001234)
  • MICR Code — Optional 9-digit code from cheque
5
Section 4 — Contact Details
Department-wise contacts — fill all four departments
  • Fill contact details for IT, Purchase, Accounts and Finance departments
  • Each department needs: Contact Name*, Designation, Email Address*, Mobile No.*
  • If the same person handles multiple departments, repeat their details in each section
  • Use official email IDs — avoid personal emails. WhatsApp-enabled mobile preferred
6
Section 5 — Declaration & Submit
Certify the form and click Submit & View
  • Enter Name*, Designation*, Date*, Email ID* of the person certifying the form
  • The OTP for email verification will be sent to this email address
  • Double-check all numbers (GST, PAN, CIN, bank account, mobile) before submitting
  • Click "Submit & View" — a 6-digit OTP will be sent to the declarant email
7
OTP Email Verification
Verify your submission via the 6-digit OTP
  • A 6-digit OTP and a PDF copy of your form are sent to the Declarant Email ID
  • Review the PDF, then enter the OTP in the popup and click "Verify OTP"
  • OTP is valid for 10 minutes — click "Resend" if needed
8
Preview & Edit
Review all submitted details on the preview page
  • Review all details, uploaded documents (GST, PAN, MSME), and contact information
  • Click "Edit Form" to go back and make corrections
  • Click "Download PDF" to save a copy
9
Confirm & Send to Email — Registration Complete
Final confirmation step
  • Tick the Confirmation Checkbox to acknowledge all details are correct
  • Click "Confirm & Send to Email"
  • A registration PDF and all uploaded documents are emailed to all registered email addresses
✅ Done! Keep copies of all uploaded certificates (GST, PAN, MSME) — you may be asked to re-submit if files are unreadable.
